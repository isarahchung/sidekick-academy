Sidekick

An agentic interface for Tower — Distyl's largest production deployment.

Distyl AI · June 2026

Context

Tower is T-Mobile's production AI customer service program.

  • Scale — One of the largest production agentic deployments in industry. Every T-Mobile customer touches it across chat, voice, and journey modalities.
  • Team — ~40 non-engineering stakeholders (care, consumer, product, conversation design) alongside a dev team. They own the quality of every interaction.
  • The gap — Making changes to the system required deep platform knowledge. Non-engineers had no direct path to improving the bots they were responsible for.
02
What we built

Sidekick gives everyone on the team a direct path to building.

  • Natural language interface — Anyone can RCA a defect, analyze production, or propose a routine change by describing what they want — no Distillery expertise required.
  • Connected to every tool — Distillery, Weave, Jira, and Databricks in one place. Cross-tool workflows that used to take hours happen in a session.
  • Proven in production — Sam, a conversation designer, built a billing journey feature end-to-end with Sidekick. Shipped with zero bugs. Two academies, ~60 people onboarded. Tower's product owner asked us to run it.

04
How it works

Safe by design.

  • Branches — Every change is isolated. Nothing touches production until it's ready.
  • Evals — All changes are tested against regression sets before merging.
  • Approvals — Every proposal requires a second set of eyes before it can merge.
